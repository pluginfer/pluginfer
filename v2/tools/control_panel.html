How Pluginfer works — end to end

  1. You run a node (this). It's an OpenAI-compatible AI gateway on your own machine.
  2. Every request is auctioned. Your node and any connected peers bid; the cheapest one that still meets your speed, quality and privacy limits wins. Your own machine wins when it's the best fit.
  3. Every answer is signed. A tamper-evident receipt proves what ran, what it cost, and who served it — nobody can fake or inflate it.
  4. Share to earn. Turn on sharing and other people's jobs can run on your machine. You earn on each one; the platform keeps a 10% commission.
  5. The ledger audits itself. Every balance must match its signed history; tampering or deletion is caught and blocks payouts. Check it yourself: audit now.
  6. Testnet now, mainnet later. Today everything is free to try. At mainnet, buyers pay real money (through a normal payment processor) and providers get paid out. No coin, no presale.
